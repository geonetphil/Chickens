Chickens

Chickens can be food, pets and a fertiliser factory. These feathery creatures are related to the flying dinosauros that became extinct millions years ago. They evolved over time to become a flightless bird. Their poop can be used as a fertiliser but the effececy is dependant on its diet. When they get old or ill, people can eat them as a main meal or chop them up and consume them as a snack.
